Old Fashioned Mac and Cheese

This is a classic recipe for macaroni and cheese. The kids will love this!

  • 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • ¼ onion, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ¼ pound processed cheese food
  • ¼ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ¼ pound shredded Swiss cheese

Steps

Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Step 2 Prepare the elbow macaroni according to package directions. Step 3 Meanwhile, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium high heat. Stir in the flour until a cream colored paste forms. Then pour in the milk and stir constantly until this comes to a hard boil, then stir for 1 more minute. Remove from heat and set aside. Step 4 When the macaroni is cooked, spread 1/2 of it into the bottom of a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Then layer 1/2 of the grated onion, 1/2 of the salt and pepper and 1/2 of each of the cheeses. Repeat this one more time: macaroni, onion, salt and pepper and cheeses, and then pour the reserved white sauce over all. Top off with small pats of butter to taste. Step 5 Cover and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
